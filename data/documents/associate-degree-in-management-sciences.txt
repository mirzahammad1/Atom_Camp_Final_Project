Associate Degree in Management Sciences
Associate Degree in Management Sciences
The Associate Degree in Management sciences is offered at the city campus (North Nizamabad branch). Associate degree is equivalent to 14 years graduation degree (BA, B.COM, or BSC degrees) as per HEC. Student may opt for the BBA degree after the associate degree by completing the remaining courses of the BBA. Student may also opt for the 16 years bachelors (BS) after finishing the Associate Degree by enrolling in the 3rd and 4th year courses and successfully completing them to get the BS degree.
