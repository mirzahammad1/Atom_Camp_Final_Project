Repository Policy
Dissemination and Preservation
The KJCIS – KIET Journal of Computing and Information Sciences encourages the wide dissemination and long-term preservation of its published scholarly content. Authors are permitted to deposit the published version (Version of Record) of their articles in institutional repositories, subject-based repositories, and personal or departmental webpages, provided that proper citation to the original publication in KJCIS is given.
Acknowledgement of Source
All deposited copies must clearly acknowledge KJCIS as the original source of publication and include a link to the article on the journal’s official website.
Relation to Archiving Policy
This repository policy operates in conjunction with the journal’s Archiving Policy, which ensures the permanent availability and digital preservation of all published content.
Open Access and Embargo
KJCIS supports open access principles and allows repository use without embargo, in line with international best practices.
