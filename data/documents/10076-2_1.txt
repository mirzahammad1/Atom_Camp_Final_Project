Transforming Higher Education: BUKC and KIET Forge Strategic Partnership
Posted at 12:14h

khadija
0 Comments

A partnership between Bahria University Karachi Campus (BUKC) and Karachi Institute of Economics & Technology (KIET), formalized on May 29, 2024. Bahria Business School Karachi played a pivotal role in establishing this collaboration.
This strategic alliance aims to leverage the strengths of both institutions, fostering the development of faculty, students, and research initiatives.
