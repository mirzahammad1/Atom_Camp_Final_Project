CoMs MUN’ 24
KIET shines at the 12th Annual College of Business Management Model United Nations! Our dedicated student delegation achieved remarkable success, with Huzaifa Iqbal (College of Management Sciences) honored with the Outstanding Diplomate Award and Muhammad Husnain (College of Computing and Information Sciences) receiving an Honorable Mention.
President AVM (R) Usaid Ur Rehman Usmani and the KIET management extend heartfelt congratulations to all participants for showcasing exceptional diplomatic skills!
The student representatives included: Ahsan Javed, Huzaifa Iqbal, Muhammad Husnain, Muhammad Muaaz, Muhammad Ammar Jaffri, Muhammad Arsam, Laiba Javed, and Ayesha Altaf.
