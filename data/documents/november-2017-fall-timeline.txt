November-2017 (Fall) Timeline.
We used to have a SAW (Students Activity Week) at KIET, looks like this November is going to be a SAM! (Student Activity Month).
Yes with full month of Activities,
KIET
​ Student Societies and Management are engaged in preparations, to bring you the following events in the month ahead:
Starting from
Flair’ 17
(Let the Hunt Begin) – 30-31 Oct. A multi-disciplinary competitions, talent hunt, indoor/outdoor games, marketing gala
All Karachi Urdu Debate Flair’17
and exhibition event with exciting prizes which shall end with an amazing Qawali Night by Faraz Sabri Qawal.
Then we have a Seminar by
Shaukat Khanum Memorial Cancer Hospital and Research Centre
on 2nd Nov, on the topic of Breast Cancer Awareness Session for female students in the Auditorium at Main Campus
From 3rd to 5th of Nov, we shall be having
KIET Model United Nations
KIETMUN-17 with the theme “Diversity | Dialogue | Development”. Along with all the essential / traditional Committees of the UN there are many new Specialized Agencies and Committees such as Arab League, UN Women and “Supreme Court of Pakistan”.
Objective of this year’s KIETMUN is inculcating a sense of vitality dialogue in our youth so that they may contribute to world to world Peace and progress amid challenges emerging out of diversities.
From 6th to 7th Nov, we have got THE ULTIMATE ENGINEERING EVENT
Student Engineering Exhibition 2017
SEE-2017. A platform for the budding and undergraduate engineers to showcase their talents in an innovative manner. With more than 18 exciting competitions in Engineering and General category, so hurry up everyone and join this mega event.
After the successful arrangement of Blood Donation Drive at Main Campus, from 6th to 12th of Nov, our other campuses will be having Blood Donation Drives by
The Indus Hospital – Blood Center
in association with
SAVE Pakistan
SPRING-18 Admission Test for all programs and An Open House for Engineering Students is also planned on 12th of November 2017 at Main Campus.
On 15th and 16th Nov, KIET brings you the very first time. An international conference at
KIET – College of Engineering
, in collaboration with
IEEE
and
IEEE Karachi Section
an “International Conference On Latest trends in Electrical Engineering & Computing Technologies.
Intellect-17
” where the international speakers are invited from China, Denmark, Malaysia, Azerbaijan and Austria along with many renowned National keynote speakers.
At City Campus we have a
Fustal Fever Fall 2017
tomorrow as well!
And in the end of November,
KIET Aviation Club – KAC
​is organizing an “RC Aircraft Design and Fabrication Workshop, which includes Lectures on basic aeronautics and RC Aircraft, Design Concept of RC Aircraft, Fabrication and Instrument installation in your own RC Aircraft and finally the testing and FLYING of your own RC Aircraft.
Have a Happy November Everyone!
