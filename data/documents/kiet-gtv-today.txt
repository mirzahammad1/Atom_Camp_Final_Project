KIET at GTV
The esteemed trio of Director COMS, Dr. Adnan Anwar, Director COE, Sir Najeeb Jafary, and HOD Electronics Engineering, Dr. Sameer Qazi, were honored guests at GTV, where they engaged in a thought-provoking discussion on the education system of Pakistani universities. Their collective expertise and insights undoubtedly shed light on crucial aspects of academia, contributing to a broader understanding of the challenges and opportunities within the educational landscape.
