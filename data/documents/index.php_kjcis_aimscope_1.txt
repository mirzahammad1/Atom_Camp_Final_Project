Aim and Scope
Aim
KJCIS aims to provide a comprehensive, multidisciplinary platform for research that advances the theoretical foundations, applied methodologies, and practical applications of computing and information sciences. The journal seeks to:
Disseminate high-quality, peer-reviewed research grounded in scientific rigor.
Promote interdisciplinary collaboration across emerging domains of computing.
Support innovation that contributes to societal growth, industrial development, and academic progress.
Bridge the knowledge gap between academia, research institutions, and industry.
Scope
The scope of KJCIS includes a broad selection of contemporary and foundational research areas. Submissions may employ theoretical, experimental, computational, empirical, or hybrid methodologies. The journal welcomes contributions in (but not limited to) the following areas:
Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning & Deep Learning
Supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning
Neural architectures, deep feature extraction, and optimization
Explainable AI, responsible AI, and fairness in algorithms
Intelligent autonomous systems
Data Science, Big Data & Knowledge Engineering
Big data processing frameworks and distributed datasets
Data mining, clustering, classification, and pattern discovery
Predictive analytics and statistical modeling
Data governance, privacy, and ethical analytics
Networks, Communication & Cyber Security
Network architecture, protocols, and performance analysis
Wireless, mobile, and sensor networks
Cryptography, key management, and secure communication
Cyber risk management and digital forensics
Software Engineering & Quality Assurance
Agile methodologies, DevOps pipelines, and CI/CD models
Software testing, verification, and validation
Model-driven engineering and software architecture
Software project management and process maturity
Distributed, Parallel & Cloud Computing
Cloud orchestration, virtualization, and containerization
Parallel algorithms and multi-core systems
High-performance computing and GPU computation
Blockchain systems and decentralized computing
Internet of Things (IoT), Embedded Systems & Cyber-Physical Systems
IoT device engineering and networked sensors
Embedded software and real-time operating systems
Smart cities, Industry 4.0, healthcare IoT
Safety-critical CPS applications
Computer Vision, Image Processing & Multimedia Systems
Visual recognition, segmentation, tracking, and scene understanding
Signal processing, compression, and reconstruction
Augmented, virtual, and mixed reality systems
Human–Computer Interaction & Natural Language Processing
User experience engineering and usability studies
Speech recognition, computational linguistics, and dialogue systems
Multimodal interfaces and intelligent interaction design
Database Systems & Knowledge Representation
NoSQL, NewSQL, and distributed database models
Semantic web technologies and ontologies
Query optimization, indexing, and transaction processing
Optimization, Algorithms & Formal Methods
Metaheuristics, evolutionary algorithms, swarm intelligence
Formal verification, correctness proofs, and specification languages
Combinatorial optimization and graph algorithms
The journal welcomes submissions from academic researchers, industry practitioners, postgraduate students, and multidisciplinary teams working on advanced computing challenges.
