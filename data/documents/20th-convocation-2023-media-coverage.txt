20th Convocation 2023 Media Coverage

EP. Report

KARACHI Institute of Economics & Technology (KIET) hold its 20th Convocation Ceremony on Wednesday, 15 March, 2023 at KIET Main Campus, Pakistan Air Force Airmen Academy, Korangi Creek, Karachi:

A total of Nine Hundred & Thirty-four graduates were conferred degrees in the disciplines. of Management Sciences, Computer Sciences, Arts, Humanities & Sciences and Engineering, The College of Management Sciences awarded 429 degrees. The College of Computing & Information Sciences awarded 363 degrees. 80 degrees were awarded to the graduates of College of Engineering and the College of Arts, Humanities & Sciences awarded 42 degrees to its graduates. Furthermore, 20 degrees were awarded to the MS/M.Phil and PhD scholars. Honorable Air Marshal (Retd.) Mr. Mirza Zafar Hussain, Chancellor KIET, was the Chief Guest at the occasion who awarded Gold and Silver Medals to the distinguished students. The Chief Guest emphasized the importance of higher education and the significance of research and development for — the progress of the country. Air Vice Marshal Hussain Ahmed Siddiqui, President Air War College Institute was the guest of honor at the ceremony.

In his welcome address, President of KIET, Air Vice Marshal (Retd.) Usaid ur Rehman Usmani_ praised the efforts of the students and faculty while emphasizing the role of education and modern research in the development of Pakistan. He thanked the Chief Guest for his gracious presence.
