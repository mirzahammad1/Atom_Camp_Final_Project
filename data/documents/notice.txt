(Notification)

RESTRUCTURING AND RENAMING OF THE “GRADUATE SCHOOL OF SCIENCES
AND ENGINEERING (GSSE)” TO THE “DIRECTORATE OF ADVANCED STUDIES AND
RESEARCH (DASR)”

Dear All,

It is hereby notified that the Graduate School of Sciences and Engineering (GSSE) has
been restructured and renamed as the Directorate of Advanced Studies and Research
(DASR), effective from Spring-2025.

Objectives of DASR:

The Directorate of Advanced Studies and Research (DASR) will serve as the central
academic and administrative body overseeing all graduate and postgraduate studies, fostering
advanced research, and ensuring compliance with institutional and regulatory standards.

Key Responsibilities of DASR:

4. Academic Oversight:
© Supervise and manage all graduate and postgraduate programs across colleges.
© Ensure adherence to quality standards for curricula, assessments, and academic
deliverables.
Before each semester, PhD Coordinators will submit a tentative plan detailing
offered courses, enrolled students, and key academic milestones (e.g, proposal
defenses, comprehensive exams, thesis defenses).
© DASR will report and seek approval from Board of Advanced Studies and
Research (BASR) for supervisor assignments, topic changes, and any supervisor
replacements,

2. Research Facilitation:
© Promote interdisciplinary research and support the publication of high-impact
scholarly work.
© Manage processes for thesis submissions, evaluations, and defenses.

3. Coordination with Colleges / Departments:
© Work closely with PhD coordinators from respective colleges / departments /
faculties for scheduling, reporting, and approvals.
© Facilitate communication between departments and the BASR

4, Coordination with Registration & Examination Department
© DASR will coordinate with examination departments for scheduling, conducting,
and reporting results of mid-term, final term and comprehensive exams.
