The IBA Branding & Advertising Conference (IBAC-8.0) held on 18-19 March, 2023 at IBA Karachi, was a fiercely competitive event that brought together some of the brightest marketing minds from across the country. And when the dust settled, it was KIET that emerged as the clear winner, producing not only the winning team but also the runner-up!
𝗧𝗲𝗮𝗺 𝟯: 𝗔𝗕𝗛𝗜, 𝗡𝗶𝗸𝗲, took home the top prize with their innovative branding and advertising strategies that impressed the judges and left their competitors in the dust. But it wasn’t just Team 3 that shone; KIET’s 𝗧𝗲𝗮𝗺 𝟭𝟮: 𝗣𝗼𝘄𝗲𝗿𝗣𝗹𝘂𝘀, 𝗚𝗶𝗹𝗲𝘁𝘁𝗲, was a close runner-up, demonstrating the depth of talent that KIET has to offer.
Attendees of IBAC-8.0 gained valuable insights and exposure to real-world challenges through presentations and discussions with industry experts and speakers. And with KIET’s impressive showing, it’s clear that the institute is producing some of the best marketing talent in the country.
“We’re thrilled with the results. This is a testament to the hard work and dedication of our students and faculty, and it’s great to see that they’re being recognized for their talent and skill. We’re proud to be a part of their journey and look forward to even more success in the future.”
