Welcome to KIET
Chancellor Message
KIET is an Institute of Higher Learning and Education. In short span of less than two decades it has progressed to be in league with top private sector universities in Sindh. The three well established and maintained campuses, fully equipped laboratories, spacious classrooms and libraries all in air conditioned environment make the process of learning very conducive. At KIET, we consider our Faculty as our core strength; a wonderful blend of both experienced and your minds whose forte remains to be out of the box thought process. They guide and channelize the true potentials of our students, equip and groom them to face the real world.
Graduates of KIET are playing an important role in many multi-national and corporate bodies, as indeed in reputed industrial units in Karachi and beyond. Scores of our former students are pursuing the banner of their alma mater in distant lands. Pakistan Air Force and Pakistan Education Foundation should be justifiably proud of their contributions towards higher learning. True assessment of any university is directly linked to the number and quality of Research undertaken by it. In this realm KIET stands amongst the top private sector universities in Karachi. KIET also provides environment for grooming and leadership training thru various extra-curricular activities, where students plan and execute their own plans with little or no guidance from the faculty, these include local sports competition, picnics and excursion to Northern Pakistan. Scuba diving at Churna island is fast becoming a favorite annual ritual.
I warmly welcome all future students to come and visit KIET and experience the feel of this Institute of higher learning before making a final choice to pursue their ambitions.
Muhammad Nasir Butt

Dear Students,
I warmly welcome you to Karachi Institute of Economics and Technology which has established itself as one of the prominent Institutions in Pakistan that Are Places to Watch in Engineering, IT Business and Media Arts Education.
As you are aware, knowledge based learning has occupied a place of prime importance and is fundamental for social and economic progress in the globalized economy. We at KIET have a conviction that the quality of a country’s human capital is critical to its development, social cohesion and global competitiveness. Therefore, the Institute and its colleges present an environment for teaching and research with a focus on entrepreneurship training, science and technology, for the industrial and socio-economic development of Pakistan.
We also work closely with professional bodies as well as industry experts in the development of our curriculum and we have consulted public and private sector organizations in the criterion of the various programs to ensure that our students are gaining relevant and requisite knowledge and skills required for a global business market and make them industry ready.
The education we will give you will be up-to-date and the inputs delivered by our faculty members who have both industry and academic experience. I am confident that your passage through this university will be one which will prepare you for a life filled with productivity and social usefulness.
Come, make use of this elaborate infrastructure filled with technology and make your career.
Best Wishes
