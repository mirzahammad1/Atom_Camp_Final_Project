PRESS RELEASE

Karachi Institute of Economics and Technology - KIET and Amadeus Pakistan
Partner to Empower Aviation Management Students through Internship Program

Karachi, July 31, 2025 — The Quality Enhancement Cell (QEC) at Karachi Institute of
Economics and Technology (KIET) has taken a significant step toward bridging the gap
between academic learning and industry exposure by signing a Memorandum of
Understanding (MoU) with Amadeus Pakistan (Premium Aviation Services).

The collaboration aims to provide final-semester students of KIET's BS - Aviation Management
program with a structured 6-week internship opportunity. Students will receive hands-on
training in airline reservation, ticketing systems, and customer service protocols using
Amadeus’ global tools and platforms. Each selected intern will also receive stipends and a
joint certificate of completion from Amadeus and KIET.

The signing ceremony was held at Amadeus Regional Office, Karachi and was attended by
esteemed representatives from both institutions.

From KIET, the following dignitaries were present.

‘meaningful learning opportunities aligned with real-world industry practices.
