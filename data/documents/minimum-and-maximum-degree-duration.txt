‘SUBJECT: MINIMUM & MAXIMUM DEGREE DURATIONS

All students shall note the following about maximum and minimum degree
durations and maximum and minimum course loads. This is for strict adherence.

1. Each academic year will commence from Fall semester and end with Spring
semester.
2. Summer semester is not a regular semester and is only for the following:
a. Repeating a failed course.
b. Repeating a course dropped.
. Only internship can be registered in Summer semester.
3, HEC has clearly defined minimum and maximum duration for each degree
program. See table 1 below for details.
4, Similarly, there are HEC guidelines for minimum and maximum courseload in
‘a semester. See table 1 below for detalls.
