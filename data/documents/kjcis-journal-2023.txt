KJCIS Journal 2023
KIET Journal of Computing & Information Sciences – KJCIS was recognize again by HEC for the year 2023-24.
That’s fantastic news! It looks like KJCIS is continuing its streak of excellence. Recognition from the Higher Education Commission (HEC) for another year is no small feat. It must be doing something right to consistently stand out in the realm of computing and information sciences.
This acknowledgment not only reflects the journal’s commitment to maintaining high standards but also showcases its relevance and impact in the academic community. It’s like getting a gold star for consistently delivering valuable insights and knowledge in the field.
I wonder what specific aspects of KJCIS caught the HEC’s attention this time. The quality of research papers, the diversity of topics covered, or perhaps innovative approaches to computing and information sciences? Whatever the reason, it’s clear that KJCIS is making a mark and contributing significantly to the academic landscape.
This recognition is not just a feather in KJCIS’s cap; it’s a testament to the hard work and dedication of the entire team behind the journal. It’s always inspiring to see institutions and publications striving for excellence and being acknowledged for their contributions. Here’s to KJCIS and another year of pushing the boundaries of knowledge in computing and information sciences!
