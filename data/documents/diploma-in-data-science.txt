Diploma in Data Science
DIPLOMA in DATA SCIENCE
DURATION:
4 months for Certificate Program
4 months for Advanced Certificate Program
4 months for Diploma Program
RULES AND REGULATIONS:
Attendance: Minimum 75% attendance
required for final exams.
Assessments: Students evaluated via quizzes,
assignments, midterms, and finals (50% passing marks).
Code of Conduct: Discipline and respect for faculty, staff, and peers are mandatory.
Certification: Certificate after 4 months, Advanced Certificate after 8 months, Diploma after 12 months.
