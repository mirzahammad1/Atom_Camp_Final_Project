The Career Development 6.0
The Career Development 6.0 was held at Karachi Institute of Economics and Technology (KIET) on April 30, 2025.
The event brought together prominent industry partners, including WALKEAZE, Adamjee Life, PharmaEVO, Mediplas Innovation, Burque Corporation, Tabba Kidney Institute, and PharmEvo, who actively engaged in a Mock Interview designed to enhance student employability and professional readiness.
During the opening ceremony, we were honoured by the presence of Mr Raza Kamal, author/writer and academician, Dr. Tariq Jalees (Associate Dean, CoMS & Acting Registrar KIET), Dr. Sajida Parveen (Director QEC) and several other senior faculty members, whose support significantly enriched the event.
Special thanks to Dr. Syeda Quratulain Kazmi for her thoughtful closing remarks and to Dr. Sofia Bano (Assistant Professor & Patron, Marion Society, CoMS) for her leadership and commitment in organizing this impactful initiative.
At KIET, we remain dedicated to bridging the gap between academia and industry, ensuring our students are equipped with the skills and confidence needed to excel in their careers.
Thank you to all our industry collaborators and participants for making this event successful.
