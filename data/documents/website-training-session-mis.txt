Website Training Session-MIS
Training Session on Departmental Website Management – 27th August
27th August 2025
, a training session was conducted for departmental representatives on managing and updating their respective websites.
The session covered:
Introduction to the main KIET website and departmental subdomains (COCIS, DASR, Admissions, QEC, ORIC).
Step-by-step guidance on editing and updating web pages.
Training on using the admin panel for making changes individually as department administrators.
Best practices for keeping content updated, relevant, and user-friendly.
The training aimed to empower departments to maintain their websites independently, ensuring timely updates and better communication with students, faculty, and stakeholders.
We thank all participants for their active involvement and look forward to
seeing enhanced departmental websites in the coming weeks.
