Successful testing of Fire Fighting Robot! – April 2018
Final testing of the fire fighting robots developed by IMR Lab was held at KIET along with the PAF firefighting team. Two robots were tested namely the wheeled mobile robot and the tread based robot. Alhamdulillah both the robots were able to carry the fire hose directly connected form the fire brigade to the affected area and extinguish fire. Complete details and video can be found at
https://imrlab.kiet.edu.pk/product-commercialization/
