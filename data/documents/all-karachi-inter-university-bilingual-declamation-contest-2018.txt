Declamation Contest’18
Press Release
KIET Holds All Karachi Inter University Bilingual Declamation
All Karachi Inter University Bilingual Declamation Contest was held on 8 th November, 2018 at the main campus of KIET. Armed with cogent arguments, several participants representing many renowned universities from across the metropolis took part and inspired the audience with their rational and emotional speeches.
Each team consisted of two speakers—one for English and another for Urdu. The teams were from PNEC-NUST, Jinnah Sindh Medical University, Jamia tul Anwar, Tabani’s School of Accountancy, Jinnah University for Women, Federal Urdu University, Dow University of Health Sciences, Institute of Space & Planetary Astrophysics, Habib University, Khadim Ali Shah Bukhari Institute of Technology, Bahria University and University of Karachi.
Habib University grabbed the best team award. In English speeches, Muhammad bin Faisal from Jamia tul Anwaar, Syed Hammad Mukhtar from Habib University, Sana Zehra from Jinnah Sindh Medical University and Wania Noor from Dow University of Health Sciences snatched first, second and third positions respectively. In Urdu speeches, Fizza Abbas from Habib University, Sub Lieutenant Waseem Ahmed from PNEC-NUST, Kainat Fatima from Jinnah Sindh Medical University and Qurat ul Ain Majeed Khan from Jinnah University for Women secured first, second, and third positions respectively.
Muhammad Ajmal Khan, Head of English Department, KIET graced the occasion as the chief guest. Panel of judges consisted of four persons. Asst Professor Shaikh Muhammad Irfan and Sohaib Ahmed Siddiqui (faculty at KIET) judged English speakers and Mufti Abdul Qadir (Religious Scholar) and Asst. Professor Sajjad Hussain (Urdu poet) judged Urdu speakers. Department of English, KIET organizes this event biannually.
