Oath Taking Ceremony
Date: 16th October 2024
Venue: KIET, North Campus
KIET North Nazimabad held an Oath-Taking Ceremony for the newly elected Student Council on October 16, 2024, alongside Teacher Salam Day to honor educators and inspire student leadership. The event celebrated integrity, unity, and academic excellence with esteemed guests and heartfelt gratitude.

Visit to Sahara Village Old Age Home
Date: 4th June 2025
Venue: Sahar Village
On June 4, 2025, the student council at KIET organized a heartfelt CSR activity at Sahara Village in collaboration with QEC, celebrating Eid al-Adha, with elderly residents through gifts, mehndi, and joyful moments. The initiative fostered compassion, cultural connection, and student engagement under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Sajida Parveen, Director QEC, and Usman Khan, faculty at KIET.

Youm-e-Tashakkur
Date: 17th May 2025
Venue: KIET, North Campus
KIET commemorated Youm-e-Tashakur with a solidarity walk and ceremony honoring the bravery of Pakistan’s Armed Forces and Shuhada, celebrating unity and national pride. The event featured speeches, patriotic performances, and heartfelt tributes by students and faculty.
