About CoMS
The College of Management Sciences (CoMS) at KIET offers dynamic business education with an entrepreneurial mindset. We prepare students for leadership roles in corporate, public, and startup environments.
With a strong focus on innovation, ethics, and industry relevance, CoMS equips students with practical skills and strategic thinking. Our faculty combines academic expertise with real-world experience to deliver impactful learning. Through research, case studies, and hands-on projects, we ensure students are future-ready and globally competitive.

Our Academic Programs
CoMS offers HEC-approved undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs designed to meet market needs.
Undergraduate Programs
A 4-year undergraduate program designed to build strong foundations in business, leadership, and innovation. Students can choose single or dual majors to align with their career goals..
GRADUATE PROGRAMS
These flexible programs are tailored for early to mid-career professionals aiming to enhance managerial and strategic decision-making skills. Weekend and evening options are available for working executives.
Associate Degree in MS
A research-oriented graduate degree focused on advanced business theories and analytical skills. Ideal for those pursuing academic, consulting, or policy-making careers.
Specialization
A rigorous doctoral program that cultivates high-level research capabilities and thought leadership. Candidates contribute original research to the fields of management, marketing, finance, and more.

Vision & Mission
Shaping ethical, innovative, and industry-ready leaders through excellence in business education and research.
vision
To become a leading institution of business education and research in Pakistan, known for academic rigor, industry relevance, and global competitiveness.
MISSION
To develop future-ready professionals who embody innovation, integrity, and leadership. We aim to deliver high-impact education through a curriculum rooted in real-world practices, supported by research, entrepreneurship, and meaningful industry engagement.
Research & Publications
The College of Management Sciences (CoMS) is dedicated to advancing knowledge through quality research, faculty development, and student engagement.
Our flagship journal, Market Forces, serves as a platform for cutting-edge research in management, economics, and business.

IRCBM – International Conference
The International Research Conference on Business & Management (IRCBM) connects academia with industry and showcases impactful research from across the globe. It provides a platform for scholars, practitioners, and students to exchange ideas, present innovations, and foster global collaborations.
