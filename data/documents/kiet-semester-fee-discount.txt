KIET Semester Fee discount
To help our students continue higher studies in the prevailing circumstances, KIET is offering 20% discount in Semester Fee of entire Program for our Alumni / Graduates taking admission in Fall-2020 for MS or PhD Program at KIET.
Please note that Admission Fee Rs 20,000/- is already waived off for KIET Graduates if they continue further studies at KIET.
