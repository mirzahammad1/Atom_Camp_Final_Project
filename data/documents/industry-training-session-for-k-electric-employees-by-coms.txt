Industry training session for K-Electric employees by CoMS
An industry training session was arranged by the College of Management Sciences on Friday for K-Electric employees. CoMS faculty members Mr. Shaikh Abdul Qadir and Mr. Salman Khan visited K-Electric 560 MW Bin Qasim Power Station 2 for a training session on the Japanese 5-S methodology. The power plant is situated at port Qasim about 40 km from the city campus.
Alhamdulillah, it was a great experience to train KE employees who mainly included GMs, DGMs, Managers, and AMs from different departments. Mr. Salman mentioned that they were hosted by their Plant head, Mr. Zubair. According to our faculty, the training session went smoothly and seamlessly. At the end of the session, participants asked a lot of questions from the facilitator Mr. Sheikh Abdul Qadir. The session concluded with an exchange of mementos between KE and KIET and certificate distribution.
Alhumdulillah we obtained extremely positive feedback from their plant head and he was eager to conduct this workshop (paid) on a larger scale in a short span of time.
Sir, we are proud that three renowned corporates (Hinopak, Peoples Steel Mills, and K-Electric) have become part of EDC-CoMS clientele.
Here we especially thank the Director City campus and Admin for providing excellent logistic support.
This is the third industry training this year by CoMS. Prior to K-Electric training, CoMS provided activities to People Steel Mills and HinoPak motors this year.
