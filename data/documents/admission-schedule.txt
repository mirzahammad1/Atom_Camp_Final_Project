ADMISSION SCHEDULE
The admission
schedule typically follows a structured timeline. It usually begins with the
announcement of admission advertisements in leading newspapers, Social Media and
on the official website. These advertisements provide detailed
information about the programs offered, eligibility criteria, and the
application process. Then Applicants are required to fill out admission
application forms which are made available both online and at the campus.
The
academic year consists of two semesters Spring (September-February) and Fall
(April-August) semesters. Its academic programs are designed to prepare the
students to meet the challenges of the business world.
Important Notice
General Instructions for Aptitude Test
Interested students must follow the general instructions for aptitude test.
The last date for receipt of application forms, duly completed, is
January 27, 2024
The Admit Card for the Aptitude Test will only be issued after the required
attested photocopies of the academic documents and I.D. Card are submitted with the admission form.
Original academics documents are also checked. Please bring your original documents for the verification purpose with the submission of your application form.
The Admit Card is mandatory for the Aptitude Test.
Applicants will not be allowed to attend the aptitude test without the admit card.
MS/M.Phil program candidates must submit the NTS-GAT results along with the admission form.
A sample test paper is available
here
Final selection will be based on the result of Aptitude Test and past Academic Performance.
