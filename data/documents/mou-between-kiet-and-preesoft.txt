MoU b/w KIET and Preesoft
Karachi Institute of Economics & Technology (KIET)
We are pleased to announce the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between Karachi Institute of Economics & Technology (KIET) and Preesoft Pvt Ltd aimed at fostering stronger linkages between academia and industry.
This collaboration will provide our students with valuable opportunities to engage with industry professionals through:
* Specialized industry-focused training programs
* Interactive knowledge-sharing sessions
* Mentorship for Final Year Projects (FYPs)
* Exposure to corporate culture, best practices, and industry expectations
At KIET, we are committed to equipping our graduates with both academic rigor and practical exposure, ensuring that they are prepared to meet the evolving demands of the professional world. This partnership reflects our continued efforts to integrate academic learning with real-world application, thereby contributing to a more skilled and innovative technology ecosystem.
Together with Preesoft, we look forward to shaping the next generation of competent and industry-ready professionals who will lead the way in future technological advancements.
