Publication Fees
Open Access Policy
KIET Journal of Computing & Information Sciences (KJCIS) is an open-access journal. All published articles are freely available online without subscription or paywall. This ensures maximum visibility and access for authors and readers worldwide.
Copyright & Licensing
Authors retain copyright of their published articles. On acceptance, authors grant the journal a non-exclusive license to publish and distribute the work. KJCIS encourages use of a standard open license (for example, Creative Commons). The specific license in use is indicated on each article page.
Article Processing Charges (APC)
Currently, KJCIS does not charge article processing or publication fees. There are no submission charges. Any future change to the APC policy will be announced well in advance and clearly stated on the website.
Self-archiving & repository deposit
Authors are permitted to self-archive their accepted and/or published articles in institutional repositories, subject to the journal’s license terms. Authors should include citation details, DOI, and a link to the published article when archiving.
Waivers & financial support
As KJCIS currently does not charge APCs, no waiver policy is required. If APCs are introduced in the future, a transparent waiver and discount policy will be implemented for authors from low-income institutions or regions.
Funding & compliance
Authors should disclose funding sources and ensure compliance with funder open access mandates. If funders require specific licensing or repository deposit, authors should indicate that in the submission.
