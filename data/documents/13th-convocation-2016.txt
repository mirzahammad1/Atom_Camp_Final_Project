13th Convocation 2016
The 13
convocation ceremony of Pakistan Air Force – Karachi Institute of Economics & Technology was held on Thursday, 21 April 2016 at the Convention Centre, PAF Museum, PAF Base Faisal, Karachi.
A total of 569 graduates were conferred degrees in the disciplines of Management Sciences, Computer Sciences and Engineering.
Governor of Sindh Honorable Dr. Ishrat-ul-Ebad Khan
was the Chief Guest at the occasion and awarded Gold and Silver Medals to the distinguished students. The Chief Guest emphasized the importance of higher education and the significance of research and development for the progress of the country.
Chief of the Air Staff, Air Chief Marshal Sohail Aman
was also present on the occasion as the Guest of Honor.
