15th Convocation ceremony-2018
The 15th convocation ceremony of was held on Friday, 16 February, 2018 at the Convention Centre, PAF Museum, PAF Base Faisal, Karachi.
A total of 698 graduates were conferred degrees in the disciplines of Management Sciences, Computer Sciences and Engineering.
Vice Chief of the Air Staff, Air Marshal Farooq Habib was the Chief Guest at the occasion and awarded Gold and Silver Medals to the distinguished students. The Chief Guest emphasized the importance of higher education and the significance of research and development for the progress of the country.
In his welcome address, President of the Institute, Air Vice Marshal (Retd) Tubrez Asif praised the efforts of the students and faculty while emphasizing the role of education in the development of Pakistan. He thanked the Chief Guest for his gracious presence.
