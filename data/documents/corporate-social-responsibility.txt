Driving sustainable impact through community engagement, ethical practices, and environmental stewardship.
Indus Hospital & Health Network Blood Drive
Date:13 Oct 2025
Venue: North Campus
Your Donation Saves Lives: Become a Voluntary Blood Donor for Indus Hospital!
The Indus Hospital & Health Network (IHHN) is dedicated to providing safe and free-of-cost healthcare, and a consistent supply of safe, voluntary blood is crucial to this mission. By donating blood, you directly support patients in need, including those undergoing complex surgeries, cancer treatment, and emergency care.
