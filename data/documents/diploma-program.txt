Diploma Program
Diploma in E-Commerce & Digital Marketing
The courses of Diploma in E-commerce & Digital Marketing include:
• Social Media Marketing
• eCommerce Website
• Amazon FBA
Module 1: Social Media Marketing (1 month, 8 class sessions)
Rs. 15950
This course will cover the following platforms:
• Brand Building
• Brand Growth
• Brand Analysis
• Brand Life Cycle
• Facebook Marketing
• YouTube Marketing
• Instagram Marketing
• Twitter Marketing
• Google Marketing
• LinkedIn Marketing
Module 2: Search Engine Optimization (1 month, 8 class sessions)
Rs. 15950
This course will cover the following platforms:
• SEO Content Creation
• Content Writing
• Designing Ad Templates
• Brand Theme Selection
• Freelancing (Fiver /Upwork)

• Order Inventory

• Trademark filing
• Inventory Inspection
• Arrange FF

• EBC
• Listing Images (Photography)

• Upload Listing Account                                                                              • Launch Account

• Track Inventory
