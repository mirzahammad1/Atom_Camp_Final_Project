KIET Research Funding of Rs 79.88 Million
𝗞𝗮𝗿𝗮𝗰𝗵𝗶 𝗜𝗻𝘀𝘁𝗶𝘁𝘂𝘁𝗲 𝗼𝗳 𝗘𝗰𝗼𝗻𝗼𝗺𝗶𝗰𝘀 𝗮𝗻𝗱 𝗧𝗲𝗰𝗵𝗻𝗼𝗹𝗼𝗴𝘆 (𝗞𝗜𝗘𝗧) 𝗿𝗲𝗰𝗲𝗶𝘃𝗲𝗱 𝘁𝗵𝗲 𝗵𝗶𝗴𝗵𝗲𝘀𝘁 𝗲𝘃𝗲𝗿 𝗿𝗲𝘀𝗲𝗮𝗿𝗰𝗵 𝗳𝘂𝗻𝗱𝗶𝗻𝗴 𝗮𝗺𝗼𝘂𝗻𝘁𝗶𝗻𝗴 𝘁𝗼 𝗥𝘀. 𝟳𝟵.𝟴𝟴 𝗺𝗶𝗹𝗹𝗶𝗼𝗻 𝗰𝗼𝘃𝗲𝗿𝗶𝗻𝗴 𝟭𝟭 𝗽𝗿𝗼𝗷𝗲𝗰𝘁𝘀
With an enhanced focus on creating an environment of innovation and research at KIET, the blooming R&D ecosystem, has greatly helped KIET researchers in winning several research grants from a diverse pool of highly competitive funding agencies.
In year 2021-22 KIET received the highest ever research funding amounting to Rs. 79.88 million covering 11 projects, these research projects will be completed in the allocated time frame of 18 to 36 months under the supervision of principal investigators from KIET. It is envisaged that these research projects will help in achieving the SDGs of Quality Education, Affordable and clean Energy, Decent work and Economic growth, good health and well-being.
Complete list of Funded Research Projects can be seen on following link:
