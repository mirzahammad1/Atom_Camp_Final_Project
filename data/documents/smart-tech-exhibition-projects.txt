Smart Tech Exhibition
KIET Avionics Engineering students recently participated in the Smart Tech Exhibition Projects (STEP) during the IEEEP Fair & Conference, hosted by Dawood University
in collaboration with Badar Expo Solution.
We’re proud to announce that our teams secured Runner-Up awards in Smart Tech for Vehicles with their Radio Controlled Aircraft and in Smart Tech for Agriculture with their Bird Repellent System! The RC Aircraft showcases impressive maneuverability, while the Bird Repellent System effectively uses green laser and acoustic sounds to protect crops.
Both teams received shields, prize money, and certificates for their remarkable efforts. The positive feedback from visitors has inspired them for future exhibitions!
Here’s to more innovation ahead!
