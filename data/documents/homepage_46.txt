Introduction
Introduction
Karachi Institute of Economics & Technology  is a degree awarding institution imparting quality education in the field of computing & information sciences, management sciences, engineering and media & arts.
College of Computing and information sciences  at KIET is working with a clear vision of producing high quality technology entrepreneurs & market leaders for national & International market. To establish a culture of research and innovation, CoCIS has taken two major steps:
Organizes a biannual conference, International Conference of Computing and Information Sciences , available at www.iccis.pafkiet.edu.pk
Publishes a biannual peer reviewed journal, KIET Journal of Computing & Information Sciences , available at www.kjcis.pafkiet.edu.pk.
ICCIS aims to provide a forum to present new research problems and recent results of the emerging technologies in the field of computing and information sciences. The conference also endeavors to act as a bridge between academia and industry and to encourage fruitful collaboration. It brings together researchers, faculty members, consultants, industry experts and technologist on one platform.
No registration fee for authors and participants to ease the impact of COVID-19 pandemic.
"This is a hybrid mode conference (Online+Physical)"

Plagiarism policy
Policy on Multiple Submissions and Prior Publication:
A submission must present original work which has not appeared/accepted elsewhere for publication, or has not been concurrently submitted to another conference/journal publication. All submitted papers will undergo plagiarism checks in multiple plagiarism detection software as mandated by IEEE. Papers found violating the above mentioned plagiarism policy may be rejected at any stage: before review, after review, after acceptance notification or before submission of conference proceedings for publishing in IEEE Xplore.
