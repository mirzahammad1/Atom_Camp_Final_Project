Editorial Board
The KIET Journal of Computing & Information Sciences (KJCIS) is governed by an experienced and diverse editorial team composed of senior academics, researchers, and industry experts. The Board provides strategic guidance, ensures editorial quality, and upholds the journal's ethical standards.
Board Structure & Roles
Patron:
Provides high-level institutional support and oversight.
Editor-in-Chief:
Responsible for editorial strategy, policy, and final decisions on manuscripts.
Managing Editor:
Oversees day-to-day editorial operations and coordination with the publisher.
Associate Editor(s):
Manage subject-specific submissions and coordinate peer review in their domains.
Academic Editorial Board:
International scholars who contribute to peer-review, special issues, and journal promotion.
Advisory Board:
Senior domain experts who advise on strategy, indexing, and quality improvement.
Production & Circulation Manager:
Oversees typesetting, DOI assignment, print production, and distribution.

Board Transparency & Best Practices
To align with international indexing standards, the journal displays for each board member (where available): full name, highest degree, primary affiliation, country, role (e.g., Editor-in-Chief, Associate Editor), ORCID iD, and a short biography (50–100 words). The journal encourages public bios and institutional email addresses to improve transparency.
Submitting Board Updates
To request updates to the board listing (corrections, new bios, ORCID, photos), editorial staff will periodically ask members to confirm their details. Any change in role or conflict of interest must be disclosed promptly to the editorial office.
Contact the Editorial Office
For queries about editorial matters, board nominations, or editorial policies, contact the Editorial Office via the journal’s contact page or designated editorial email address.
