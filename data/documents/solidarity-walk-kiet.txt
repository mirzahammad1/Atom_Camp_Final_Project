Solidarity Walk-KIET
In a powerful display of unity and compassion, the KIET community organized a Solidarity Walk to step up for Palestine, sending a resounding message of support and empathy. Students, faculty, and staff joined hands in a peaceful procession, raising awareness about the ongoing challenges faced by the Palestinian people. The event not only emphasized the importance of solidarity but also fostered a sense of global responsibility within the KIET community. As participants marched with purpose, their collective steps echoed a call for justice, peace, and understanding in a region that has long grappled with complex issues. The Solidarity Walk stands as a testament to KIET’s commitment to social awareness and its dedication to being a catalyst for positive change on both local and global scales.
