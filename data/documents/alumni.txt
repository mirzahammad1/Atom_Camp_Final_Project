Our Services
Corporate Relations Department
Our dedicated team in Corporate Relations Department is always ready to maintain strong interaction with all our graduates to facilitate variety of benefits and services. Alumni play a vital role in job placement, internships and other educational activities of our students and graduates. Alumni record is periodically updated to provide opportunities. In order to strengthen relations with our alumni, we annually organize alumni dinner in which alumni and their spouses are invited.

Meet the Alumni
Alumni speaker sessions are conducted every semester where KIET Alumni who have continued successful career are invited to share their experience, pitfalls and the practicalities of the professional world  as well as guide the students in working and preparing for it. Hence, the students, especially the ones who are about to step into the professional world, could get an insight about how to face the challenges, learn new things and tasks, deal with the co-workers and superiors, and make decision for their first career.
