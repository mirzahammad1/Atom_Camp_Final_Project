Management Information System
MIS SERVICES
The Management Information Systems (MIS) Department of Computer Services is dedicated to making the KIET operate more efficiently through the use of the software. MIS supports the University’s computing needs by writing custom software, investigating the system, and identifying how existing software can be augmented or used more effectively.

Employee Portal
KIET LMS
KIET LMS is an online Learning Management System that provides a common, easy-to-use framework for faculty & Students that offers all required options/processes to be used during the semester.
All the current semester students and teachers are automatically enrolled in their registered courses at KIET.

Quality Enhancement Cell
qEC
The Higher Education Commission (HEC) has made a policy decision to direct all Higher Educational Institutions (HEIs) to establish. It has also issued Guidelines for the establishment and monitoring of QECs.
QEC attempts to survey all aspects of university functioning, bringing them at part with international standards. QEC ensures that KIET fulfills its mission and objectives.

Our Services
Support
KIET offers its services to students, passing out to graduates, alumni, and the corporate sector with an aim to connect them to a diversified result-oriented talent pipeline.
International Collaboration
The objective of the Office of International Collaboration (OIC) is to develop and execute collaborative partnership agreements with all leading universities around the world.
Corporate Relations
The objective of Corporate Relations Department is to provide guidance to the institute towards offering job-oriented programs and contributing to the development of the industry.
Alumni Support
Our dedicated team in Corporate Relations Department is always ready to maintain strong interaction with all our graduates to facilitate variety of benefits and services.
KIET Library
This is a multi-disciplinary learning space serving the faculty, students, researchers and staff of the university facilitating Research and Scholarship.
Sports Complex
KIET organizes inter & Intra-Campuses competition tournaments of Cricket, Volleyball, Football, Hockey, Basketball and Table Tennis (Boys/ Girls).
Transport Services
KIET provides its students with an efficient and reliable system of transportation on affordable rates to enable them to commute with comfort.
