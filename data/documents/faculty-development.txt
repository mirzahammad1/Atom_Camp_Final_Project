Faculty Development
Empowering learning through innovative pedagogy, technology integration, and Outcome-Based Education to drive academic excellence
Live Webinar on Qualitative Research
Date: 22nd May 2025
Venue: KIET City Campus
Department: CoMS
KIET hosted an insightful webinar on Qualitative Research on led by Dr. Junaid Ansari, Associate Professor at IoBM, focusing on methodologies, data collection techniques, and ethical practices in social sciences. The session engaged faculty and researchers, encouraging the adoption of qualitative approaches in academic research.

Panel Discussion on 'AI and the Changing Landscape of Education'
Date: 24th May 2025
Venue: KIET, North Campus
An engaging panel discussion on “AI and the Changing Landscape of Education” was held on May 24, 2025, at KIET North Campus, featuring Anila Chaudhary NLP Practitioner, Bilal Hameed CEO ALT Academy, and Abdul Wasay Head of The Lab School , and moderated by Erfa Iftikhar Educationalist & a Certified Cambridge Trainer. The session explored how AI is transforming the future of education and learning.

Panel Discussion on 'Are We Trapped in the Web of Fake News?'
Date: 10th May 2025
Venue: KIET, North Campus
KIET QEC hosted a compelling panel discussion on titled “Are We Trapped in the Web of Fake News?” featuring prominent journalists and experts who explored the impact of misinformation and emphasized the importance of media literacy and responsible journalism. The session encouraged critical thinking and student engagement on navigating truth in the digital age.

Avalanche Connect with Decentralized Technologies
Date: 21st May 2025
Venue: KIET, North Campus
KIET North Campus hosted “Avalanche Connect,” a seminar organized by Decentralized Technologies in collaboration with Avalanche on May 21, 2025. International experts Doro Unger Lee and M. Usman Qureshi shared insights on blockchain and the decentralized future.

Avalanche Connect with Team 1
Date: 11th March 2025
Venue:  Auditorium, Department of Pharmacy, UoK
The Department of Mathematics at KIET, in collaboration with the University of Karachi, organized “Ava1anche Connect with Team 1” featuring expert talks on blockchain technology. Mr. Usman Quershi and Mr. Muzammil shared insights on platforms like Ethereum and Polygon, encouraging student involvement in the blockchain community.

International Seminar on Strategic Decision Making
Date: 13th March 2025
