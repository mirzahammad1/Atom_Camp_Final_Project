Lecture on Quaid-e-Azam
We were honored to host Air Commodore Khalid Chishti (R) at the Karachi Institute of Economics & Technology for a distinguished guest lecture. His journey of reviving the legacy of Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah is truly inspiring. In his lecture, he shared the unseen and untold influences of Quaid-e-Azam, highlighting the values that continue to inspire our youth today.
Over the years, he has dedicated himself to restoring and colorizing rare portraits of our founding father, coupled with powerful narratives that showcase Jinnah’s selflessness and devotion.
We extend our gratitude to the KIET faculty, management, and students for being present at this enlightening session. Thank you, Air Commodore, for your valuable insights and commitment to inspiring our youth with the ideals of our national heritage!
