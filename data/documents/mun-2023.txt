About MUN:
Model United Nations is an academic simulation of the United Nations where students play the role of delegates from different countries and attempt to solve real world issues with the policies and perspectives of their assigned country. Once a team has registered for a conference, it receives a country to represent. Each student on that team will represent that country in a different committee with different topics.
About KIETMUN:
The legacy of an event is the long-term positive impact that it has on the institution whether it is culturally, economically or physically. Model United Nations also known as Model UN or MUN, it’s an academic competition and education simulation that enables participants in staying effectively updated about current events, International relations, and diplomacy in form of a simulation of the United Nations. The Concept of Model United Nations was brought up by the United Nations to build and prepare the young generation as future leaders. Its various committees KIETMUN’22 act as a platform for youths all across the world from diversified regions and backgrounds to be involved in exchanging their distinctive ideas for effective global policymaking. Taking place in one of the world’s biggest megalopolises, the inaugural conference is expected to be attended by the best and brightest minds of today’s youth. Through these conferences, participants learn key tools like effective communication, multi-lateral diplomacy and professionalism, in addition to becoming informed citizens of the global populace. Karachi Institute of Economics and Technology Model United Nations is a simulation of the United Nation. The first edition of KIEMUN was held in 2013 in which KIETMUN gave the young leaders a platform to showcase their potential. We are now here one more time with an aim of a much bigger event, KIETMUN 2023. We believe in our young leaders and we want to give them a stage once again so that they can showcase their public speaking and leadership skills. The participants discuss and debates on the current world affairs which helps them to enhance their knowledge which will take them too much bigger forums but most importantly, it’ll make them learn and have an experience which will take them to better places. KIETMUN is a great platform for learning and taking experience for all the participants. Speaking with the previous experiences of the successful events that we have had, also KIETMUN gives opportunities to the youth to participate in and learn about conferences, debating, polishing their thinking capabilities, negotiating and last but not the least, it will transform them into a leader as they will be gaining all the skills from the experience. Our youth is our power and there is a leader that resides in each and every one of us. Now is the time to discover that within us through this amazing opportunity of Karachi Institute of Economics and Technology Model United Nation 2023.
