Panel Discussion on 'AI and the Changing Landscape of Education'
Date: 24th May 2025
Venue: KIET, North Campus
An engaging panel discussion on “AI and the Changing Landscape of Education” was held on May 24, 2025, by Quality Enhancement Cell (QEC), featuring Anila Chaudhary NLP Practitioner, Bilal Hameed CEO ALT Academy, and Abdul Wasay Head of The Lab School , and moderated by Erfa Iftikhar Educationalist & a Certified Cambridge Trainer. Panel Discussion on 'Are We Trapped in the Web of Fake News?'
Date: 10th May 2025
Venue: KIET, North Campus
KIET QEC hosted a panel discussion on titled “Are We Trapped in the Web of Fake News?” featuring  journalists Faizullah Khan (Senior Journalist, ARY News), Ajmal Malik (Editor, Nawashta-e-Deewar), Shahid Ali (Senior Economist), Akhtar Ali Shaheen Rind (Senior Journalist and Anchor), Prof. Dr. Irfan Aziz (Mass Communication from FAUSST ) and Azmat Ali Khan, (Senior Journalist) who addressed the dangers of misinformation. The session highlighted the need for media literacy, critical thinking, and ethical journalism in today’s digital landscape.

Youm-e-Tashakkur
Date: 17th May 2025
Venue: KIET, North Campus
KIET commemorated Youm-e-Tashakur with a solidarity walk and ceremony honoring the bravery of Pakistan’s Armed Forces and Shuhada, celebrating unity and national pride. The event featured speeches, patriotic performances, and heartfelt tributes by students and faculty.

Visit to Sahara Village Old Age Home
Date: 4th June 2025
Venue: Sahara Village
The student council at KIET organized a CSR activity at Sahara Village in collaboration with QEC, celebrating Eid al-Adha, with elderly residents through gifts, mehndi, and joyful moments. The initiative fostered compassion, cultural connection, and student engagement under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Sajida Parveen, Director QEC, and Usman Khan, faculty at KIET.
