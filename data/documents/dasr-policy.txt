Directorate of Advanced Studies and Research (DASR): A Policy
Document

1, Structure of Graduate Studies
The Directorate of Advanced Studies and Research (DASR) has been formed, effective in
Spring 2025. Its members include PhD coordinators of COMs, and COCIS. DASR would be
headed by the director advanced studies and research who would be a senior permanent faculty
‘member. The president KIET will head the committee.

2. Broad Function
The broad function of this office is to recommend and report the cases to BASR as required;

Specific Functions
1. Before the beginning ofa new semester, PhD coordinators of each college will send a
tentative plan to the DASR. It will contain the following information:
a, The courses that the college will offer in the semester.
b. The names of the students appearing in the comprehensive exams with tentative dates
€. The names of the students who are expected to appear for proposal defense with
tentative dates (if applicable)
4. The names of the students who are expected to appear for pre-final PhD thesis defense
with expected dates.
e. The names of the students who are expected to appear for the final PhD thesis open
defense with expected dates.

2. Reporting Allocated Supervisors/ Change of the Supervisors/Change of Disciplines

a. DASR will report the allocated supervisors to BASR for approval.

b. Ifthe supervisor is changed, the directorate will report the same to BASR for approval
before the final defense

¢. Ifthe topic/supervisor of a PhD student changes after the proposal defense, it needs to
be reported by DASR and sent to BASR for approval.

4. Students need to clear the GAT subjective exam of the same discipline. In case of
change of discipline (for cases before 2023), GAT has to be re-taken

e. No inter-disciplinary transfer is allowed in PhD after 2023, as per HEC policy.

3. Comprehensive Related activities

a. A week before the comprehensive exam, the coordinator will inform the DASR about
the comprehensive exam date.

b. The college coordinator will also send the comprehensive examination paper to the
examination department one week before the exam. The examination department will
conduct the comprehensive examination and send the examination answer sheets to the
respective coordinator.

4, Proposal Defense-Related Activities

defense to BASR.

5. Thesis Defense Related Activities

‘After receiving the final thesis and plagiarism report from the supervisor, the college
coordinator will arrange a mock defense. In mock defense, the college coordinator will
invite two internal examiners who will evaluate the quality of the thesis.

After receiving satisfactory reports, the college coordinator will send the final thesis to
the head of DASR with the names of three potential examiners. The Presidenthead of
DASR will select any two foreign evaluators and send them the thesis along with the
pre-developed thesis evaluation evaluator form to foreign evaluators.

Upon receiving the foreign evaluator reports, the President/head of DASR will pass
them on to the DASR.

DASR will review the reports. If the reports are satisfactory, the college coordinator
will pass the report to the supervisor.

Thereafter, the student and supervisor will address the changes.

DASR, in coordination with the PhD Coordinator, will arrange a date for the PhD open
defense and finalize one external, one internal, and one DASR nominee examiner.

The college coordinator will send the thesis to the viva examiners.

Upon successful defense, the PhD coordinator will send the reports to DASR and the
supervisor.

The student and supervisor will address all suggested changes.

The DASR will report the names of the students to BASR. The DASR will only state
the thesis title, date of open defense, and the supervisor's name to BASR.

The PhD coordinator will ensure that the student file is complete in all respects and will
send it to the examination department for final scrutin
