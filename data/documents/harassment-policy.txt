KIET has zero tolerance for any form of sexual harassment that interferes with the
work performance of a student or an employee. The instructions provide guidelines
to safeguard incidents involving the violation of personal dignity that is when
done physically, verbally or electronically and is a punishable offence.

SEXUAL HARASSMENT SHOULD NEVER BE TOLERATED
Students can use cell phones in class, only with teacher's permission. Cameras will
be watching students, if rules are broken they shall be punished.
A complaint may be lodged either by e-mail or in person meeting with the head
or any focal person of the inquiry committee.
Minor Penalties
 Monitory fine or course/semester drop or change of campus.
Major Penalty
 Rustication from KIET.
Physical
 Unwanted touching, brushing, gestures, kissing,
hugging, rubbing shoulders/back or other abnormal behaviors
of an intimate nature.
Visual
 Sending sexual images/videos, unwelcome gestures, &
staring at someone else's body offensively.
Psychological
 Psychological harassment is defined as negative or hostile
behavior by one or more persons, directly or indirectly targeting
a third person.
Verbal
 Verbal comments, "jokes", remarks, sexual questions, etc.
Mobbing also is an VERBAL unethical or destructive way of
reacting to a situation or behaving towards a person.
Stalking
 Repeatedly following someone, unwanted contact,
spreading rumors, spying, leaving unwanted gifts, repeated calls
or texts, hacking social accounts.
