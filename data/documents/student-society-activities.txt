Student Society Activities
Activities
Students are encouraged to be involved with extracurricular activities on campus.   Part of the education experience is to happen outside of the classroom.  KIET offers a rich variety that offers a chance to meet others with similar interests. The Computer Science students at KIET are engaged in a number of different activities:
IEEE
Engineering/Computing Technology Society
IEEE is a professional association advancing innovation and technological excellence.  IEEE and its members inspire a global community to innovate and develop  through its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities. IEEE is a trusted standard for engineering, computing and technology information.
