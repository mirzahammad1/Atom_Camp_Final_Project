Empowering Future Innovators
Welcome to Karachi Institute of Economics & Technology (KIET) – a future-focused university committed to excellence in technology, business, engineering, and media sciences.
Recognized by HEC and PEC, KIET offers an industry-aligned curriculum, modern facilities, and a vibrant campus life to prepare students for real-world success.

Explore Our Academic Programs
Degrees That Lead to Careers
KIET offers a rich variety of academic programs designed to meet the demands of today’s job market. Our engineering and computing programs are backed by specialized labs and research facilities, while our management and media programs foster creativity, innovation, and leadership. Whether you aim to build software, design aircraft, manage a business, or produce digital content, KIET provides the structure, support, and mentorship you need. Faculty members with real-world experience ensure your learning goes beyond theory. Our academic environment encourages critical thinking, collaboration, and entrepreneurial spirit.
Scholarships & Financial Support
Making Quality Education Accessible
KIET is deeply committed to supporting deserving and talented students through its scholarship and financial aid programs. Various financial support options are available, including need-based assistance, merit-based scholarships, and discounts for siblings and alumni. Students facing financial challenges are encouraged to apply early and discuss available support with the admissions team. Our goal is to ensure that financial limitations never stand in the way of quality education. Every year, hundreds of KIET students benefit from these programs and graduate with pride and dignity.
Investing in Your Potential
We believe that financial limitations should never stand in the way of talent and ambition.
Flexible Scholarship Options
Merit-based, need-based, and special category scholarships are available for eligible students.
Supporting Every Background
From sibling discounts to aid for differently-abled students, KIET supports diverse financial needs.
Apply Early, Maximize Benefits
Early applicants receive priority consideration for financial assistance and scholarship programs.
Campus Experience
A Place That Inspires Growth
The main campus, located at Korangi Creek, is a purpose-built facility with modern architecture and serene surroundings. Classrooms are equipped with multimedia tools and air-conditioning, while specialized labs serve engineering, computing, and aviation disciplines. The campus features an extensive digital library, cafeterias, student lounges, and sports facilities to support student well-being. Students can also engage in clubs, competitions, and cultural events that bring campus life alive. The environment at KIET encourages curiosity, confidence, and a strong sense of community among its students.
Why KIET?
A University That Cares About Your Future
Students at KIET benefit from a strong academic foundation combined with career development and global exposure. Our curriculum is reviewed by industry leaders to ensure it remains relevant and impactful. Internship opportunities, corporate visits, seminars, and job placement support are integral parts of student life. We are proud to have a growing network of alumni working in top national and international organizations. At KIET, you are not just a student — you are part of a lifelong learning journey guided by mentorship and purpose.
How to Apply
A Simple and Streamlined Process
Applying to KIET is quick and easy through our online admissions portal. Start by creating an account, fill in the application form, and upload your documents. Once the application is submitted, our team will guide you through the next steps, including the entry test or interview if required. Applicants are encouraged to submit their applications early to take advantage of available scholarships and priority processing. Support is available through phone, WhatsApp, and email in case you have any questions during the process.
