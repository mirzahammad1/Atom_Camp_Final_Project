Logistics & Supply Chain Management
It is a discipline concerned with the efficient flow of people and goods through the industrial and economic system.  Logistics management is that part of the supply chain, which applies the systems approach to the management of a wide variety of activities, e.g. Purchasing, inventory control, distribution, traffic management, location analysis, packaging and customer service.
