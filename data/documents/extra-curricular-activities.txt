Eid Fest 2025 | مشاعرہ & Annual Dinner
Date: 10th April 2025
Venue:  North Campus
KIET North Nazimabad Campus hosted a vibrant Eid Fest 2025, featuring a soulful *مشاعرہ*, heartfelt poetry, and a joyful gathering of faculty and staff. The evening celebrated culture, connection, and community, ending with a grand dinner and tokens of appreciation for esteemed poets.

All Karachi Inter University Bilingual Declamation Contest
Date: 25th May 2025
Venue:  North Campus
KIET North Nazimabad Campus hosted the All Karachi Inter University Bilingual Declamation Contest (Spring 2025) featuring 48 participants from 24 universities. Organized by the Department of English, winners in English and Urdu categories received cash prizes for top positions.
