KIET’s Independence Day
KIET’s Independence Day event featured a dignified flag hoisting ceremony, a cake cutting, and inspiring speeches alongside patriotic songs. An engaging quiz competition added to the excitement. The occasion was honored by the presence of the President of KIET, Heads of Departments, staff, and students, all coming together to celebrate our nation’s heritage.
