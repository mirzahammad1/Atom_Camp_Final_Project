Industry Visits
Industry Visit to Lucky Textile Mills Limited
A Journey from Academia to Industry: A Visit to Lucky Textile Mills!
On May 6th, 2024, the students of Karachi Institute of Economics and Technology (KIET) participated in a mock interview preparation session at
Lucky Textile Mills Limited
, in collaboration with the HR department. Under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Sajida Parveen, the PSD-2 class underwent CV preparation, interview practice, grooming, and personal development as part of their course outline. To bridge the gap between theory and practice, the students were then taken to Lucky Textile Mills Ltd for real-life assessments.
Before the mock interview session, the students received a briefing on industry practices and operations. Mr. Muhammad Ahsan Paracha, Advisor to the Management, graced the occasion, emphasizing the significance of personal development in the industry. Ms. Sidra Mahmood Jafri, Head of HR, provided valuable guidance to the students. Ms. Meerub Javed delivered a presentation on personal and professional development, with a focus on communication skills, attitude, and effective idea dissemination.
KIET extends its gratitude to Lucky Textile Mills Ltd for hosting this event.
Industrial Visit at Siddiq Sons Limited
Industrial visits play a vital role in shaping the careers of students pursuing professional degrees. As an integral part of the university curriculum, these visits aim to provide students with firsthand insights into the internal workings of companies. Going beyond the confines of textbooks, industrial visits offer students a practical perspective.
In line with this, the students of OB and Entrepreneurship class, supervised by Dr. Sofia Bano, embarked on a visit to Siddiq Sons Limited on May 04, 2024. The visit granted them a unique opportunity to witness the global denim and garment industry in Pakistan. It proved to be an excellent platform for students to gain comprehensive awareness of industrial policies and practices of the textile industry.
