About ORIC-KIET
About the Office of Research, Innovation & Commercialization (ORIC)
The Office of Research, Innovation & Commercialization (ORIC) at KIET fosters a dynamic ecosystem for high-impact research, innovation, and industry partnerships.
KIET, chartered by the Government of Sindh in 2000, serves over 4,000 students and 9,000+ alumni through its three colleges: Engineering, Management Sciences, and Computing & College of Computing and Information Systems.
ORIC is building a vibrant ecosystem to transform research into real-world impact.
A Thriving Research Environment
With a team of 27+ PhD-qualified faculty members and growing research collaborations, KIET’s research culture continues to flourish. Numerous research projects have recently been funded by the Higher Education Commission (HEC), reinforcing our national relevance.
Our programs
Management
Research in the College of Management Sciences emphasizes real-world business innovation through areas like Marketing, Finance, Human Resource Management, and Supply Chain Management, contributing to both academic and industry growth.
Computer Science
The College of Computing and Information Systems focuses on emerging technologies such as Cloud & Grid Computing, Data Science, Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, Software Engineering, and the Internet of Things (IoT), addressing modern digital challenges.
Engineering
KIET’s College of Engineering conducts cutting-edge research in Intelligent Robotics, Embedded Systems, Signal Processing, Smart Networks, Electronics, and Aerodynamics. These areas are driven by advanced technologies and led by experienced PhD faculty
KIET’s vision for research & innovation
KIET is establishing its ORIC at a transformative point in its R&D journey. Having already built a strong foundation in research and development, we now focus on identifying and eliminating barriers that hinder innovation and research commercialization.
Advancing Research Support
ORIC at KIET is dedicated to strengthening the university’s R&D by streamlining access to research grants and ensuring ethical research practices.
Innovation & Entrepreneurship
We empower faculty and students to lead innovative projects while encouraging a startup culture through incubation and entrepreneurial support.
Industry Links
ORIC bridges the gap between academia and industry by facilitating patents, partnerships, and the commercialization of research outcomes.
Our Mission & Vision
Mission
To strengthen the research culture at KIET by encouraging innovation, commercialization, and collaborations that serve national development.
Vision
To be recognized as a hub of impactful research, cutting-edge innovation, and academic-industry synergy in Pakistan and beyond.
