KIET Talent hunt and Support (KTS)
An Open House for prospective candidates of “KIET Talent Hunt & Support Program” (KTS) was organized by
KIET – College of Engineering
today on 26 July, 2017.
Open House started with formal introduction of different departments and programs offered by CoE followed by a campus tour. Parents and guardians of the students also visited Institute’s laboratories, library and other facilities. We thank all the attendees of Open House for their visit to our campus.
KIET has announced
KIET Talent hunt and Support (KTS)
for all Engineering Programs at KIET. KTS Aptitude test and an Open House is planned on
23rd August, 2017
. KTS Classes will Inshallah start from
28th August, 2017
at Main Campus. Result of first KTS test can be viewed at
kts.kiet.edu.pk/news/
Eligibility for KTS
Students with Pre-engineering or A-levels (with Physics, Maths and Chemistry) are eligible to apply. Very limited seats are available for DAE students.
Minimum percentage requirement in Pre-engineering, A-levels and DAE is 60%.
Those awaiting second year result can also apply.
Benefits for students to attend Talent hunt program are summarized below.
Scholarship during KTS.
Scholarship during four-years Engineering degree.
Exact scholarship details can be found at
https://kts.kiet.edu.pk/kts-details/#d6
Training to start engineering education.
Get a glimpse of semester-based system and engineering education.
Judge your caliber for engineering education
in 16 weeks
Be part of our search for talented students who can undergo state of the art engineering education:
KTS program is a not for profit program, rather it is being substantially funded by KIET.
More details about KTS program can be found at
https://kts.kiet.edu.pk/kts-details/#d2
For application procedure, please visit
https://kts.kiet.edu.pk/apply/
If you have any queries about KTS, feel free to email at
kts@kiet.edu.pk
Open House
Open House
has also been planned to take place at College of Engineering (CoE) KIET (Main Campus) on
23rd August 2017
. Prospective engineering students and their parents are invited to attend Open House session. During this half a day activity, students and their parents will attend presentations about CoE and KIET, visit campus (labs, classrooms, other facilities), and will participate in academic competitions and demonstrations.
If you are interested to attend open house, please fill the following short form.

Scholarships announced – July 2017:
Top 10% students who join KTS Fall 2017 will be awarded 85% tuition fee waiver, next top 10% will be awarded 40% fee waiver in KTS fees. Top 10% students who successfully complete KTS studies will be awarded need-cum-merit scholarship (upto 85%) for entire 4 years engineering degree. Exact scholarships details can be found at
https://kts.kiet.edu.pk/kts-details/#d6
