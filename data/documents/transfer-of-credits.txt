TRANSFER OF CREDITS
"A student who wishes to obtain information about transfer from another college to KIET College of Media & Arts may make an appointment with Director of the college. The student should submit a transcript of all courses completed or in progress."
KIET is a chartered institution and accepts credits from all HEC recognized institutions under the following conditions:
A candidate must obtain 72 marks or B grade in the equivalent course to apply for transfer/ exemption at KIET.
A candidate may request for transfer of 4 maximum courses in the master degree program at KIET.
A candidate may request for transfer of 8 maximum courses in the 4 years bachelor degree program at KIET.
The transfer/ exemption of course is only granted if the course has same credit hours and matching course content as the corresponding course at KIET.
Level of degree shall be same, i.e. A bachelor level course completed elsewhere does not make one eligible for exemption for Master coursework.
Course and credit hour exemptions are only offered if a student is applying in the same degree at KIET.
If a student has completed the same course in a different degree earlier, the academic content of that course may be transferred if the course content is matched. In this case, student must do substitute course/s to fulfill the credit hour requirement.
Exemption/transfer in courses is not offered in MS and M.Phil degrees at KIET.
Student may be tested through examination process at KIET for every course in which one is claiming exemption to check the level of knowledge before the course and its credit hours are transferred.
Course exemption fee is charged on courses exempted/transferred. Fee of Rs. 1500 per exemption shall only be charged if course and corresponding credit hours are transferred.
Internship and project courses are excluded from transfer/ exemption.
