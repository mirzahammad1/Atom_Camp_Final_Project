16th Convocation 2019
The 16th Convocation Ceremony of KIET was held on Tuesday, 19 February, 2019 at the Convention Centre, PAF Museum, PAF Base Faisal, Karachi.
Honorable Governor of Sindh Mr. Imran Ismail was the Chief Guest at the occasion who awarded Gold and Silver Medals to the distinguished students. Deputy Chief of The Air Staff (Training) Air Marshal Shahid Akhtar Alvi was the guest of honor.
Ceremony was largely attended by parents and guests from Industry & Academia. A total of 675 graduates were conferred degrees in the disciplines of Management Sciences, Computer Sciences, Engineering and Media & Arts.
