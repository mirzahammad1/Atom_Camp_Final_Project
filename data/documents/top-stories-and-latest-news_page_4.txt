Dated: 22/04/2024

To,
The Chairman,
Higher Education Commission.

Respected Sir,

The Pakistan Hindu Council (PHC), in order to fulfill its social responsibility for playing its due role in uplifting socio-
economic conditions of youth through reducing unemployment, talent showcasing, career growth, and transforming
university students into an employable workforce, is organizing the PHC Job Fair & Education Expo on Monday, May
27th, 2024 at Korochi Expo Center in hall no 6, Karachi that aims to connect job seekers with potential employers all
‘over the country. We believe that this job fair would provide a valuable opportunity for university students/graduates
to explore career options as well as organizations to recruit well-qualified talented individuals.

The job fair, with the support of the Higher Education Commission (HEC), State Bank of Pakistan and our other partner
organizations, will take place over the course of one day, with booths set up facilitating HR / Recruitment professionals
related to diverse backgrounds/sectors such as government / semi-govt autonomous bodies, banking, technology,
healthcare, hospitality & tourism, media houses, marketing and consultancy, etc. to showcase their organizations, job

‘openings, internships, and other career opportunities.
