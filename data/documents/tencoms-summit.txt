On Friday 24th December 2021, Karachi Entrepreneurship Summit was organized by The Entrepreneurial Network of Collage of Management Sciences (TEN CoMS) a growing Business Incubator & Accelerator at KIET. Renowned startup founders, incubation experts & young change makers were part of the three main Panel Discussions.
Summit was started with the welcome note of Dr.Tariq Jalees, Senior Professor & Associate Dean CoMS (Collage of Management Sciences) followed by Key Note speech of Honorable Registrar M. Nizam Uddin, (R) Air Commodore.
Main highlight of Karachi Entrepreneurship Summit was its 3 disruptive panel discussions in which dynamic personalities from all over Karachi were invited. In first panel discussion which was moderated by Ms Rozina Imtiaz in-Charge TEN CoMS. In Panel Mush Punjwani Founder & Chief Happiness Officer Coffee Waghira, Imran Azhar Founder & CEO AZ Corp, Aarij Hussain Director BIC-IU & Andeel Ali Monitoring & Evaluation Head NIC Karachi shared there valuable insights & experiences either as a founder of successful startup or as a contributor for Startup ecosystem.
In the second panel discussion, Ahmed Rauf Essa Founder & CEO Telemart, Syed Moin Ali Head of Technology Business Incubation Center at ICCBS, Ahsan Jabbar CEO A J Ventures, Khurram Kalimi Founder & CEO Vinn Corp shared their remarkable experiences. This panel was also moderated by Ms. Rozina Imtiaz In-Charge TEN CoMS.
For the final Panel Discussion, TEN CoMS introduced a new opportunity for young change makers. Young enthusiasts & founders of GEN Z who are creating impact in society were invited, In the Panel of GEN Z Talks Saad Allahwala Youngest Chapter Director Startup Grind, Hamza Raza Co-Founder Funooni & Founder at Astola Digital, Laraib Javeed Founder Floraison & Amna Nadeem Founder & CEO Digital Multiverse shared their journey as a GEN Z founder. The Panel was moderated by the Student Body President of TEN CoMS Mudasir Zafar Iqbal.
