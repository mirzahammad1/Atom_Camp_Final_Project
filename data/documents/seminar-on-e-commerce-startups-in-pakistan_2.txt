On Thursday 31st march 2022, a full day Seminar was organized by The Entrepreneurial Network of College of Management Sciences (TEN CoMS) a growing Business Incubator & Accelerator at KIET.
Renowned social entrepreneurs, social activists, incubation center & E-commerce experts were part of Seminar.
Seminar was started with the welcome note of Prof. Dr. Tariq Jalees, Associate Dean CoMS (College of Management Sciences). Main highlight of the Seminar was its 5 Guest Speakers in which renowned &  profound personalities from all over Karachi were invited.
In first half Samiya Hameed Founder & President talluQ NGO, lecturer of Entrepreneurship at Iobm,Ruby Rahman a renowned social activist & Founder of Hunarmad Welfare Organization and lastly Nisha
Rao Transgender Lawyer, Social advocate & human rights activist shared their valuable insights & experiences either as a founder of successful social enterprise, their life experiences & struggles and
how they created impact.
In the second half of the Seminar which was solely on E-commerce & emerging technologies, our guests were renowned industry experts, Dr. Gulfam Executive Director Extreme Commerce College & Lead of
Extreme Commerce Incubation Network and Hassan Syed Founder of Bir Ventures & Idea Gist, Former Convener of CSR committee on Innovation & Entrepreneurship (FPCCI) enlightened students of KIET
regarding emerging technologies & development of entrepreneurial mindset.
Seminar ends with the thanking note of Ms. Rozina Imtiaz, In-Charge TEN CoMS & Senior Lecturer at KIET.
