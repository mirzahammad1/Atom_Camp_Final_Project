KIET Sports Fest 2025
𝗞𝗜𝗘𝗧 𝗦𝗽𝗼𝗿𝘁𝘀 𝗙𝗲𝘀𝘁 𝟮𝟬𝟮𝟱 | 𝗔 𝗖𝗲𝗹𝗲𝗯𝗿𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻 𝗼𝗳 𝗣𝗮𝘀𝘀𝗶𝗼𝗻 𝗮𝗻𝗱 𝗣𝗲𝗿𝗳𝗼𝗿𝗺𝗮𝗻𝗰𝗲!
From thrilling cricket and football matches to intense badminton, table tennis, and athletics — our students showed incredible spirit and teamwork across all events!
The closing ceremony was graced by Dr. Shahida Shoaib, Ex-Producer Sports PTV, as the Chief Guest. In her inspiring address, Dr. Shahida emphasized the importance of sports in character building and personal development. She lauded KIET’s efforts in providing students with a platform to showcase their talents beyond academics.
