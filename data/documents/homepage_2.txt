About the Department of Computer Science
The Department of Computer Science at KIET offers a dynamic learning environment focused on innovation, research, and real-world impact. With a strong foundation in theory and hands-on practice, we prepare students for the future of technology through state-of-the-art labs, industry-driven curriculum, and experienced faculty.

Message
Welcome to the College of Computing & Information Sciences (CoCIS) at KIET. As President of this esteemed institution, I am proud to lead a dynamic academic environment where innovation, research, and excellence converge. At CoCIS, we are committed to equipping our students with the knowledge, skills, and ethical grounding necessary to thrive in the rapidly evolving digital world. With a focus on emerging technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, Data Science, and Software Engineering, our programs are designed to meet global standards while addressing local challenges. As a PhD scholar and lifelong advocate for quality education, I believe in nurturing not just competent professionals, but visionary leaders who will shape the future of technology in Pakistan and beyond. I invite you to explore the opportunities that await you at CoCIS and become a part of our journey towards academic and technological excellence.
Dr. Khalid Khan
President, KIET
Meet Our Faculty
Our faculty includes PhD scholars and industry veterans with expertise in Machine Learning, Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, and more. Their mentorship transforms students into professionals ready to lead.
The strength of the College of Computing & Information Sciences (CoCIS) lies in its distinguished and diverse faculty. Our team comprises experienced professors, PhD scholars, and industry professionals dedicated to academic excellence, cutting-edge research, and student mentorship. With expertise in fields such as Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, Software Engineering, and Data Science, our faculty members bring real-world relevance to the classroom and foster a culture of innovation, ethics, and collaboration.
