20th Convocation 2023
Karachi Institute of Economics & Technology (KIET) hold its 20th Convocation Ceremony on Wednesday, 15 March, 2023 at KIET Main Campus, Pakistan Air Force Airmen Academy, Korangi Creek, Karachi.
A total of Nine Hundred & Thirty-four graduates were conferred degrees in the disciplines of Management Sciences, Computer Sciences, Arts, Humanities & Sciences and Engineering.
The College of Management Sciences awarded 429 degrees. The College of Computing & Information Sciences awarded 363 degrees.
80 degrees were awarded to the graduates of the College of Engineering and the College of Arts, Humanities & Sciences awarded 42 degrees to its graduates.
Furthermore, 20 degrees were awarded to the MS/M.Phil and Ph.D. scholars.
Honorable Air Marshal (Retd.) Mr. Mirza Zafar Hussain, Chancellor KIET, was the Chief Guest at the occasion who awarded Gold and Silver Medals to the distinguished students.
Air Vice Marshal, Mr. Hussain Ahmed Siddiqui, President, of Air War College Institute, was the Guest of Honour of the Ceremony.
The Chief Guest emphasized the importance of higher education and the significance of research and development for the progress of the country.
In his welcome address, the President of the Institute, Air Vice Marshal (Retd.) Usaid ur Rehman Usmani praised the efforts of the students and faculty while emphasizing the role of education and modern research in the development of Pakistan.
He thanked the Chief Guest for his gracious presence.
Congratulations to all the Graduates!
