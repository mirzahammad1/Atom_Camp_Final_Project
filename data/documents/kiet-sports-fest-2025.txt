Let the games begin!
Under the banner of sportsmanship, KIET proudly launches its Sports Fest! From badminton smashes to cricket strikes, from the roars of victory to moments of pure perseverance — this is more than just a competition; it’s a celebration of energy, unity, and passion.
“𝙇𝙚𝙩’𝙨 𝙘𝙝𝙚𝙚𝙧 𝙛𝙤𝙧 𝙚𝙫𝙚𝙧𝙮 𝙚𝙛𝙛𝙤𝙧𝙩, 𝙚𝙫𝙚𝙧𝙮 𝙫𝙞𝙘𝙩𝙤𝙧𝙮, 𝙖𝙣𝙙 𝙚𝙫𝙚𝙧𝙮 𝙢𝙤𝙢𝙚𝙣𝙩 𝙤𝙛 𝙥𝙚𝙧𝙨𝙚𝙫𝙚𝙧𝙖𝙣𝙘𝙚!”
Whether you play, support, or organize — YOU are a part of the game.
Let’s make this week unforgettable!
