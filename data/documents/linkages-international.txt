MoU signed between KIET and DOW
Date: 21st April 2025
Venue: DOW, DUHS, Ojha Campus
Karachi Institute of Economics & Technology (KIET) and Dow University of Health Sciences (DUHS) signed a stategic MOU on April 21, 2025. Spearheaded by KIET’s Quality Enhancement Cell (QEC), this MoU reflects a strong commitment to interdisciplinary growth, academic innovation, and quality-driven collaboration.
This forward-looking alliance brings together DUHS’s expertise in health sciences, management & research with KIET, creating a powerful synergy to address emerging educational and business industry challenges.
Key objectives of the MoU include:
• Interdisciplinary Course Development: Jointly designed and delivered programs integrating business and health sciences.
• Faculty & Student Exchange: Cross-institutional exposure to broaden academic and cultural perspectives.
• Collaborative Research Initiatives: Focused projects addressing real-world issues & challenges through expertise lens.
• Academic Event Co-hosting: Webinars, workshops, and conferences for knowledge sharing and professional development.
The signing ceremony was graced by notable leaders including Prof. Dr. Mohammed Saeed Qureshi (T.I), Vice Chancellor of DUHS, and Prof. Dr. Muhammad Khalid Khan, President KIET, alongside Prof. Dr. Tariq Jalees, Registrar KIET, Dr. Ashar Afaq, Registrar DUHS, Dr. Shahid Qureshi, Rector, GIFT University, Prof. Dr. Asima Faisal, Director IBHM, Dr. Sufyan Ramish, Program Director IBHM, and Prof. Dr. Sajida Parveen, Director QEC KIET.
Together, DUHS and KIET are redefining academic collaboration—merging disciplines, inspiring innovation, and advancing Pakistan’s higher education landscape.
KIET & CIMA's Exemption Partnership
Date: 16th July 2024
Venue: Korangi Creek Campus, KIET
On July 16, 2024, the Karachi Institute of Economics & Technology (KIET) formally entered into an exemption partnership agreement with the Chartered Institute of Management Accountants (CIMA UK). This strategic collaboration enables students enrolled in BS Accounting and Finance, BBA, and MBA programs at KIET to pursue the CGMA Finance Leadership Programme (FLP) and work toward achieving a globally recognized CIMA qualification.
Under this partnership, eligible graduates will receive thirteen exemptions out of a total of sixteen courses required for the CIMA qualification. Students can obtain full CIMA membership upon successful completion of three essential case study examinations.
This agreement not only validates the academic strength and industry alignment of our programs but also provides students with a significant pathway to professional success in the field of management accounting and finance.
