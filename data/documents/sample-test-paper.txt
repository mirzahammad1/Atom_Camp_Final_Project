SAMPLE TEST PAPERS
General Instructions
Applicants must follow the following  instructions for aptitude test.
1. Mark your answer at the appropriate space.
2. There will be no negative marking.
3. Follow the instructions of the test controller and stop editing when you are told to do so.
4. Calculators / gadgets of any kind are not allowed.
5. Mobile phones are not allowed in the examination venue.
6. All the questions are self-explanatory. Do not ask for any clarification.
