Welcome to KJCIS
KIET Journal of Computing & Information Sciences (KJCIS)
is a bi-annual, multi-disciplinary,
Y-Category HEC recognized
research journal published by the
College of Computing & Information Sciences (CoCIS), Karachi Institute of Economics & Technology (KIET), Pakistan.
The journal publishes high-quality peer-reviewed research in all major domains of computing and information sciences. KJCIS serves as a global platform connecting researchers, scientists, practitioners, and industry experts.
Key Features
Bi-annual publication
(June & December)
Double-blind peer review
process
Open Access (free for authors & readers)
No article processing or publication fee
Indexed
by Crossref, EuroPub, WorldCat, DRJI, and others
Strong academic Editorial and Advisory Boards

Publication Ethics
KJCIS adheres to the highest standards of publication ethics in accordance with guidelines from COPE (Committee on Publication Ethics). We maintain strict policies against plagiarism, data fabrication, and duplicate publication.
Transparency
Clear review and publication processes for all stakeholders.
Author Rights
Protection of intellectual property and proper attribution.
Rigorous Review
Double-blind peer review by domain experts.
