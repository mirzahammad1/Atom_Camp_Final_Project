Self- Institutional Performance Evaluation (IPE)
Self IPE of Karachi Institute of Economics & Technology was conducted on
14 June 2019
. Director QEC and Deputy Director QEC Indus University, Karachi were external evaluators.  While visiting various departments of the Institute external evaluator  Director QEC Ms. Sheeba  Haider appreciated the maintenance of greenery and especially the lawns/sports Fields grass. She also commended the Research Labs, Sports Activities, and Infrastructure & Facilities of the Institute. She advised setting/maintaining the record of the University Portfolio Report (UPR) in accordance with HEC spelled out criteria. Action has been taken to make amendments and set things as directed in IPE Evaluation Report.
