SCHOLARSHIP & FEE DISCOUNT
KIET Academic Scholarships
KIET offers a comprehensive academic scholarship system based solely on merit. This system aims to motivate students, encourage academic excellence, and reward high performance each semester. The institute awards scholarships to 7% of its total student population, currently benefiting over 100 students.
Scholarship amounts range from 20% to 85% tuition fee rebates. Students with at least 75% in their board exams can apply for scholarships in their first semester at KIET.
