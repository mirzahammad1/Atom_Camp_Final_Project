Parking & Transportation
“Safe, reliable, and affordable travel — connecting KIET students across the city.”
KIET provides convenient and reliable transport facilities to ensure safe and comfortable travel for students across the city. The transport service connects all campuses with major residential and commercial areas, making daily commuting easier and more affordable.
Buses and vans operate on fixed routes with scheduled timings, allowing students to reach campus on time and return home without hassle. The service is managed by the university’s transport office, which regularly monitors routes, safety standards, and overall efficiency.
Through this facility, KIET aims to support students in focusing on their studies and campus life without worrying about transportation challenges.
Key Details
Routes Covered:
Major areas of Karachi including Korangi, North Nazimabad, Gulshan, Shahrah-e-Faisal, and nearby localities.
Timings:
Morning pick-up and afternoon/evening drop-off aligned with class schedules.
Eligibility:
All registered students can apply for transport services.
How to Avail:
Students may contact the Transport Office to register for the service and obtain route details.
Contact:
Transport Office, KIET North Campus – faizan@kiet.edu.pk
