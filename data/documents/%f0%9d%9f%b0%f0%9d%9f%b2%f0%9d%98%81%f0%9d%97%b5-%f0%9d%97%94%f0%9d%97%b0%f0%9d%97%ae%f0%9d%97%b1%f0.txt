46th Academic Council Meeting
The 𝟰𝟲𝘁𝗵 𝗔𝗰𝗮𝗱𝗲𝗺𝗶𝗰 𝗖𝗼𝘂𝗻𝗰𝗶𝗹 𝗠𝗲𝗲𝘁𝗶𝗻𝗴 of the Institute was held on Wednesday 06 July 2022 at the Main Campus in hybrid mode (on-campus and online via Zoom).
President KIET chaired the meeting. Despite rainfall, the meeting was attended by almost all members of the Council. Additional Secretary Education, Govt of Sindh, representatives from the Pakistan Air Force, and the Pakistan Education Foundation attended the proceedings. During the meeting, Strategic plans for Colleges, changes & up-gradation of curriculum, and other academic-related matters were discussed and finalized.
