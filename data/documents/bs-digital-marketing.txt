BS Digital Marketing
BS – Digital Marketing
BS – Digital Marketing program is designed to develop students for acquiring knowledge in the emerging field of Digital  Marketing . The course content is focused on inculcating and developing required skills through academic text and cases as well as hand on projects and latest tools in classroom environment and in the real world. The courses of the Digital Marketing offers students and understanding of Digital Marketing, social marketing and e-commerce that are the backbone of the modern marketing approach.
