BS Business Analytics
BS Business Analytics program is designed to develop students for acquiring knowledge in the emerging field of data analysis that is the backbone of business strategy development for current and future success of the organization . The course content is focused on inculcating knowledge and developing required skills through academic text and cases as well as hand on project and latest tools in classroom environment and in the real world. the courses of the BS Business Analytics offers students the knowhow of data collection methods and developing analytical  and predictive skills to confidently forecast for future direction of the business specifically and for the industry in general.
