Directorate of Advanced Studies & Research (DASR)
With a mission to be a research-led university, KIET has a rapidly developing and vibrant research ecosystem consisting of world-class researchers, facilities, stakeholder engagement, and administrative support system.

Our Mission & Vision
Elevating postgraduate studies through quality, innovation, and impact.
Established in Spring 2025, DASR serves as the central academic and administrative body overseeing all MS and PhD programs at KIET. We coordinate research, supervise academic standards, and promote multidisciplinary collaborations to position KIET as a hub for research excellence in Pakistan.

Graduate Programs Supervision
Elevating postgraduate studies through quality, innovation, and impact.
DASR ensures quality in curricula, assessments, and research across all faculties. From course planning to thesis defenses, we provide transparent, well-structured academic pathways, enabling students to achieve their academic and professional goals.
Driving Knowledge Beyond Boundaries
Collaborative research for a better future.
We promote high-impact interdisciplinary research across fields like AI, sustainable development, healthcare, and cybersecurity. Our scholars and faculty are encouraged to publish, innovate, and contribute to national and international research landscapes.
Research Enterprise

Research Conferences
KIET organize individual research conferences specifically from Engineering, Management & Computing Sciences departments.
ICCIS-CoCIS
International Conference on Computing & Information Sciences
To establish the research culture among the students and faculty members, KIET sort out a global gathering, International Conference on Computing & Information Sciences (ICCIS). To produce quality professionals by using diverse learning methodology, aspiring faculty, innovative curriculum and cutting edge research, in the field of computing & information sciences
INTELLECT-CoE
International Conference On Electrical Engineering
The aim of this conference is to bring together researchers, scientists, academicians and representatives of industry to discuss the latest directions of research in engineering technologies, identify solutions to current national problems through industry-academia collaboration and to contribute in nation building.
IRCBM-CoMS
International Research Conferences on Business Management
The aim of IRCBM is to provide platform for educators, Researcher, Academicians, Scholars, Managers, Graduate students and Entrepreneurs from different culture backgrounds to present and discuss researches, development and innovation in the field of Business Management and Entrepreneurship.
